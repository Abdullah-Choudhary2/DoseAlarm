Our Plan

1. Executive Summary:

DoseAlarm is poised to revolutionize medication adherence with our flagship product, the DoseAlarm Timer. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health by providing innovative solutions for medication management. The DoseAlarm Timer is a unique device that tracks the last time a medication bottle was opened and alerts users when it's time for their next dose. Our leadership team brings together expertise in healthcare technology and product development, positioning us for success in this growing market. We are seeking funding to accelerate product development, expand our market reach, and achieve our growth targets.

2. Company Description:

DoseAlarm is dedicated to addressing the widespread issue of medication non-adherence by providing convenient and effective solutions. Our DoseAlarm Timer serves individuals managing chronic conditions, daily medication regimens, and caregivers seeking to ensure their loved ones' health. Our competitive advantage lies in our innovative product design, user-friendly interface, and focus on improving patient outcomes. With a team of experienced professionals in healthcare, technology, and business management, DoseAlarm is well-positioned to make a significant impact in the medication adherence space.

3. Market Analysis:

The healthcare technology market is experiencing rapid growth, driven by increasing demand for solutions that improve patient outcomes and reduce healthcare costs. DoseAlarm targets a large and underserved market segment of individuals requiring daily medication management. Competitive research has shown the limitations of existing medication reminder devices and apps, highlighting the need for our unique tracking and alarm features. Our market analysis indicates strong demand for our product, with potential for expansion into related healthcare sectors in the future.

4. Organization and Management:

DoseAlarm is structured as a Corporation with CEO, Ronald Ganzorig, at the helm, bringing his expertise in medical technology. Our leadership team includes experienced professionals in healthcare technology, product development, and marketing, ensuring a well-rounded approach to business strategy. An organizational chart detailing key roles and responsibilities is provided in the appendix, along with resumes and CVs of our team members.

5. Product Line:

DoseAlarm's primary product is the DoseAlarm Timer, a medicine bottle timer that tracks medication usage and provides timely reminders. Designed to improve medication adherence, the DoseAlarm Timer benefits users by reducing the risk of missed doses and associated health complications. Our product lifecycle includes ongoing research and development to enhance features and functionality, as well as potential expansion into complementary healthcare solutions.

6. Marketing and Sales Strategy:

Our marketing strategy focuses on digital channels, including online advertising, social media, and partnerships with healthcare professionals and organizations. We aim to raise awareness of the DoseAlarm Timer's benefits and target specific demographics within our market segment. Sales will primarily occur through our website and strategic retail partnerships. Monthly sales projections and marketing expenses are detailed in the financial projections section.

7. Funding Request:

DoseAlarm is seeking $5,000 in funding to support product development, marketing initiatives, and operational expenses. The funds will also facilitate market expansion and strategic partnerships to drive sales growth. We are open to both debt and equity financing options, with a clear plan for fund allocation and future financial sustainability.
